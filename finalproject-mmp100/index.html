What is a Mandatory Minimum Sentence?

Mandatory Minimum sentences is a sentence that was enacted to convict someone automatically with a a required minimum amount of incarceration for a crime, no matter if it was there first or last offense? It also prohibits a judge discretion in sentencing.

There are many people that are against Mandatory Minimums.
